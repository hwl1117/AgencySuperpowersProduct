# VideoBrain Design System

Premium dark theme for intelligent knowledge base.

## Design Philosophy

"Let knowledge shine like gems on dark velvet"

- Dark canvas (#09090b) as system anchor
- Purple gradient (#7c3aed) as brand accent
- 4-level surface hierarchy for depth
- Generous whitespace for elegance
- Refined micro-interactions

## Color System

### Primary
- Primary: #7c3aed (purple)
- Primary Hover: #8b5cf6
- Primary Pressed: #6d28d9

### Surface
- Canvas: #09090b (deepest)
- Surface 1: #18181b (cards)
- Surface 2: #1f1f23 (elevated)
- Surface 3: #27272a (dropdowns)

### Text
- Ink: #fafafa (primary)
- Ink Secondary: #d4d4d8
- Ink Muted: #a1a1aa

## Typography

- Font: Inter
- Display: 48px, weight 700, tracking -1.8px
- Body: 15px, weight 400, line-height 1.6

## Components

### Buttons
- Primary: purple bg, white text, 12px radius
- Secondary: transparent, border
- Ghost: transparent, muted text

### Cards
- Surface 1 bg, 16px radius, hairline border
- Hover: stronger border, enhanced shadow

### Inputs
- Surface 1 bg, 12px radius, 48px height
- Focus: purple border, glow shadow
